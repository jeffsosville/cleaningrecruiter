What We Do

Focused on professional and leadership positions: operations managers, regional directors, account executives, general managers, VPs, and C-suite roles in cleaning companies.

Who We Are

CleaningRecruiter is built by Jeff and John Sosville, founders of Sosville Group, bringing over 20 years of deep industry expertise in service business operations, marketplace building, and data infrastructure.

We've built successful vertical platforms before. Our ATM Brokerage became the dominant marketplace in the ATM industry by systematically building infrastructure that didn't exist—aggregating listings, connecting buyers and sellers, and creating the professional network that defined the space.

Now we're applying that same approach to talent in the cleaning industry. Instead of trying to be recruiters, we're building the platform infrastructure—aggregating job listings from across the web, organizing them by role and specialization, and creating the first comprehensive career resource exclusively for cleaning professionals.

Our network spans 60,000+ cleaning companies and 1,500+ business brokers across the industry. We understand the operational realities of commercial cleaning businesses, the career paths that exist within them, and the talent gaps that companies struggle to fill.
